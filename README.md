# Pulsephone Sales Analysis Report (2017–2024)

A data analysis project examining Pulsephone's smartphone sales performance across brands, models, stores, price ranges, and hardware specifications, built with **Microsoft Excel**.

![Pulsephone Dashboard](Screenshot%202024-10-25%20at%2020.18.21.png)

---

## Project Overview

This report analyses Pulsephone's sales data from 2017 to 2024, identifying which brands, phone models, RAM configurations, battery capacities, and sales channels drive the most revenue.

**Areas covered:**
- Brand and phone model performance
- Store and channel comparison
- Price range analysis
- Battery capacity and RAM impact on sales
- Year-on-year sales trends

## Key Findings

| Metric | Best Performing | Lowest Performing |
|--------|----------------|------------------|
| Brand | Samsung | Oppo |
| Phone Model | Samsung Galaxy Z Fold | Apple iPhone 15 Pro Max |
| Store | Amazon DE | OnePlus |
| Price Range | Medium | Low |
| Battery | 5000 mAh | 4800 mAh |
| RAM | 8 GB | 3 GB |
| Year | 2023 | 2017 |

---

## Recommendations

**Brand Strategy**
- Samsung should maintain leadership through continued innovation and strong marketing.
- Oppo should enhance product features, adjust pricing, and strengthen its online presence to close the gap.

**Phone Models**
- Continue promoting the Galaxy Z Fold's premium features.
- Apple should investigate iPhone 15 Pro Max's underperformance and adjust its marketing and feature positioning.

**Sales Channels**
- Amazon DE should leverage its product variety and customer service strengths.
- OnePlus needs improved marketing and a more competitive product lineup.

**Pricing**
- Focus promotional efforts on the medium price range as the highest-converting segment.
- Reassess value perception for low-priced models to reduce reliance on the bottom tier.

**Hardware Specs**
- Highlight 5000 mAh battery benefits in marketing; use customer feedback to improve the 4800 mAh offering.
- Promote 8 GB RAM devices as the performance standard; investigate demand barriers for 3 GB models.

**Year-on-Year Growth**
- Analyse the 2023 strategies that drove peak performance and systematically apply those learnings to future planning.
