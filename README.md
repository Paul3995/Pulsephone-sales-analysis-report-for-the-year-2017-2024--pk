# Pulsephone Sales Analysis (2017–2024)

This project looks at Pulsephone's smartphone sales over seven years, breaking down performance by brand, phone model, store, price range, battery capacity, and RAM to understand what's actually driving sales.

## What I Found

Samsung was the best performing brand across the period; Oppo was at the bottom. The Samsung Galaxy Z Fold led all phone models while the Apple iPhone 15 Pro Max surprisingly underperformed. Amazon DE was the strongest sales channel, with OnePlus at the lowest end.

In terms of specs, phones with 5000mAh batteries and 8GB RAM consistently outsold their alternatives. Medium-priced devices were the sweet spot — low-priced models didn't convert as well as you might expect. 2023 was the peak year for sales; 2017 was the lowest.

## Recommendations

Samsung's dominance isn't accidental — continued innovation and strong marketing have kept them ahead. Oppo needs to do more than just adjust pricing; a real look at product features and how they're communicating value online would help close the gap.

Apple's iPhone 15 Pro Max underperformance is worth investigating closely — whether it's a pricing, feature, or messaging issue, something isn't landing with buyers. The Galaxy Z Fold's success gives Samsung a strong flagship story to keep telling.

For accessories and lower-performing specs like 3GB RAM and 4800mAh batteries, the data suggests customers either don't see the value or aren't being told the right story. Better bundling deals and clearer comparisons could help.
